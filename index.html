Frequently AskedQuestions

How to resolve Internet connection error on your Dami Vibe POS Terminal

By default, your Dami Vibe POS terminal comes with an internet-enabled sim card, which it uses to open the Dami Vibe app and process transactions. Whenever there is an internet connection issue on the device, the Dami Vibe app will display an error

What are the supported industries and categories on Dami Vibe?

When filling the Compliance form on your Dami Vibe Dashboard, you must select the appropriate Industry and Category for your business. In the Dami Vibe context, an Industry refers to the broad, general sector in which you operate your business. On the other hand, a Category is a more specific classification that provides more insight into the goods and services for which you will be receiving payments through Dami Vibe.
